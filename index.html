My Case Study

Social Traveler

Social Media and rolled into one simple and easy to use package

My Research

I interviewed 5 individual and evaluated their responses on their thoughts on technology.

Problem Statement

Staying in touch with friends and family while planning a trip is a difficult task.

With the information I gathered, I created a user persona with unique scenarios and goals and his path on how he uses the NSURE App

My new streamlined process will allow users to create their event and share, invite and keep their friends and family in the know of their travel plans all in one app
